About us

Our flour and grain factory is located in Jakarta, Indonesia and has been in operation for many years. We pride ourselves on producing high-quality flour and grains for both domestic and international markets. With over 400 skilled workers, we are able to meet the demands of our customers and ensure that our products are of the highest standard. We are committed to using sustainable and eco-friendly practices in our production process, and we are constantly looking for ways to improve our operations. We are proud to be a leading supplier of flour and grains in Indonesia and we look forward to serving you.

Our products

Our flour and grain factory specializes in producing a wide range of high-quality flours and grains. We use only the finest ingredients to ensure that our products are of the highest standard. Our wheat flour, for example, is made from the best quality wheat and is perfect for making bread, pastries, and other baked goods. All our products are packed in a clean and hygienic environment and are suitable for a wide range of customers, including households, food manufacturers, and retailers. Our products are also halal certified.

Work with us

At our flour and grain factory, we are always looking for talented and dedicated individuals to join our team. We offer a variety of positions in production, quality control, logistics, and administration, as well as opportunities for career development and advancement. To apply, please submit your resume and we will review your application and contact you if you are selected for an interview.
